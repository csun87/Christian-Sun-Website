About Me

Hi! My name is Christian, and I'm a computer science student at the University of Pennsylvania with minors in music and mathematics.

I love computers and have been programming since I was twelve-years-old. Through my experiences from growing up in Singapore to attending Penn, I have developed a wide range of interests such as software development, data science, and quantitative finance. I have experience from previous internships, research, and personal projects in these fields, and I am constantly seeking to work on these interests.

Some of my hobbies outside of programming include music, roller coasters, and keyboards. In high school, I was one of the top clarinetists in the nation; I have always had an affinity for music. I am also a roller coaster enthusiast and have ridden 141 roller coasters so far. My current typing setup is a NK65 Entry keyboard with Tangerine switches.
